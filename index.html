The White Horse Inn

We have a dedicated room block at White Horse Inn for the whole 3-day weekend (Friday, Saturday and Sunday nights, Sept 2-4). There is a 2-night minimum. Room rates for our group will be $99/night plus taxes & fees for one person in a room. It’ll be $15/night for each additional person in a room. To book, you must CALL the Inn at: (802) 496-9448 to reserve a room in the room block before July 2, 2022. After that date, rooms will be released to the public. Note - they’re holding all 26 rooms for us up until that date!

Address: 999 German Flats Road, Waitsfield, Vermont 05673
Phone: (802) 496-9448
Distance from Venue: 2.3 miles
Website: www.whitehorseinn-vermont.com

West Hill House B&B

We have arranged a special discounted price at West Hill House B&B, one of the top rated B&Bs in the area about 15 minutes from the wedding venue at the Mad River Barn. Book online at westhillbb.com and use the promotion code “K&J” for any three or more nights between Wednesday August 31st and Monday September 5th 2022. (Note the 3-night minimum!) Rooms are subject to availability, and must be booked by May 31st 2022 to receive the discounted rate.
